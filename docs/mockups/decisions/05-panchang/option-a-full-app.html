Festivals & Observances
Vinayaka Chaturthi
Monthly observance of Lord Ganesha on Shukla Chaturthi. Auspicious for worship, new beginnings, and removing obstacles. Fasting from sunrise to moonrise is recommended.
Shukla Chaturthi
Skanda Sashti
Sacred day dedicated to Lord Murugan (Kartikeya). Special prayers and pujas are performed at Murugan temples. Devotees observe a fast and break it after evening prayers.

Design Decision Analysis

Full panchang experience. Competes with Drik Panchang and AstroSage. Huge draw for daily active users -- Indians check panchang every morning. But requires: maintaining festival database, regional variant support, accurate astronomical calculations for each location. Significant ongoing data maintenance.
