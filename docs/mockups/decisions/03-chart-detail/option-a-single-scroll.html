All chart data presented on one long scrolling page. Sections flow vertically: chart visualization, planetary positions, house details, aspects, divisional charts, yogas, and dasha periods. Users scroll through everything in sequence.

Pros: Simple mental model -- everything is on one page, no hidden data. Easy to implement. Works well for printing/export. Natural for users who want a complete overview. Good for sharing chart details as a single document.
Cons: Very long page (2000+ px). Can feel overwhelming with so much dense astrological data at once. Hard to find specific information quickly. Users lose context of the chart visualization while scrolling through data tables below. No way to compare sections side-by-side.
Best for: Users who prefer completeness over focus. Print/export scenarios. Simpler implementation. First-time visitors who want to see everything their chart contains.
