Option B: North Indian (Diamond) Format
North Indian (Diamond) format. Houses are FIXED (1st house always top-center). Signs move based on Ascendant. Standard in North India and most Kundli software. Reading direction is counterclockwise from the top. More familiar to traditional astrologers who learned the Parashari system. The diamond geometry is slightly harder to implement in CSS but creates a distinctive, recognizable visual.
