Decision 2 — Final: Dropdown with Icon Previews
Dropdown with icon previews lets users switch chart formats without leaving the page. Each option includes a pure-CSS mini icon providing instant visual recognition of the chart shape — the South Indian grid, the North Indian diamond, the East Indian rectangular grid, and the Western circular wheel. The selected format shows a checkmark and gold highlight. "Set as default" at the bottom remembers the user's preference. The tradition and format dropdowns are placed in the top bar for quick access without taking space from the chart. The tradition dropdown selects the astrology system (Vedic, Western, Chinese, Hellenistic, Mayan, Celtic) and the format dropdown changes based on the selected tradition (e.g. Vedic shows South/North/East Indian; Western shows Circular Wheel). Smart default is auto-detected based on the user's language and location setting.
