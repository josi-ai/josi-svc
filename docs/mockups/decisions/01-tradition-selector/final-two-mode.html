Decision 1 -- Final: Two-Mode Tradition Selector

Users choose how they want to experience traditions. Both Option A (global switcher) and Option B (per-section tabs) had clear strengths, so the final design offers both as user-switchable view modes. A subtle segmented control at the top of the content area lets users toggle between Focus View and Explore View.

This respects different user intents: sometimes you want deep immersion in a single tradition, other times you want cross-tradition discovery. The mode choice persists in user preferences.

Focus View
One tradition at a time, full immersion. A prominent tab bar at the top of the content area switches ALL dashboard content to the selected tradition. Best for practitioners who work deeply within one system.
Explore View
Mix and match traditions per section. Each card has its own inline tradition tabs. View Vedic sky data while looking at a Western chart. Best for learners and cross-tradition explorers.
